Commodity Transport

Fuel Detection & Usage Monitoring System

A commodity trading company managing 10–20 vehicles across multiple subcontracted transport partners had no reliable way to monitor fuel consumption. Losses were accumulating undetected every month — with no way to identify where, when, or with which partner.

20+Vehicles tracked
WksTo first detection
3–5Partners monitored

What was built

A Python-based fuel monitoring API connected to a real-time web dashboard. Fuel issued vs consumed, distance vs usage, time-based trip analysis, and automated exception alerts — all running across every vehicle and every partner without manual input.

Outcomes delivered

  • Fuel leakage detected within weeks of go-live — for the first time in the operation's history
  • Full cost visibility across every vehicle and every transport partner simultaneously
  • Partners held accountable against objective, real-time data — not estimates
  • Ongoing exception reporting flags anomalies before losses compound
